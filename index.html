What do we do?

We’re a GTA5 / FiveM Roleplay Community, based in Hounslow, West London. We roleplay the multitude of emergency services that operate in the London area, including the Metropolitan Police, British Transport Police, London Ambulance Service, London Fire Brigade.

We operate patrols almost every day of the week from 7.30pm until around 10pm (GMT/BST). This gives our members the chance to put their extensive training into use. We’re not a 24/7 economy / life themed server.
